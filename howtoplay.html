The Sound Game

Listen to the mystery sound and try to guess what it is by clicking on the icons.

  • Use the "Filter" options to select sound categories you want to play with.
  • Click "Start Game" to begin. A sound will play automatically.
  • Click on the sound icon you think matches the mystery sound.
  • If you guess correctly, the icon disappears, and a new sound plays.
  • If you guess incorrectly, Try again!
  • Your score and the time elapsed are displayed.

Sound Explorer

Explore and play all available sounds.

  • Use the "Filter" options to narrow down the sounds by category.
  • Click on any icon to hear its sound.

ASMR Keyboard

Record and play ASMR sounds.

  • Use the "Filter" options to narrow down the sounds by category.
  • Click on any icon to hear its sound.
  • Click on an icon/sound and place it on the pad.
  • Choose a time frame for the loop.
  • Click "Record" to start recording.
  • Play the sounds by using the keyboard and record them onto the loop.
  • The loop will keep running and recording as long as you play the sounds.
  • Click "Stop" to save the loop.
  • You can download the loop as a .wav file.
